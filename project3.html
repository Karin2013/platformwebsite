Reflection as artwork

For this project, We created a pair of 3D sculptures as our platform as refelction based on our research. This pair of sculptures is a combination of digital and manual works, which is divided into two spaces. One represents the negative aspect of light, and the other is the expression of the ideal state of light.

Resonating With Light (By Karin & Vivian)

Ladder of real estate (By Junha)

Physical Sculpture: Resonating With Light (By Karin & Vivian)

We chose to create a 3D sculpture based on our research. We made a cube that has multiple shapes flowing out of one of the corners. This represents the ideal state of coexistence with light. We made organic shapes that demonstrates life sources. This particular corner is shown as a flourishing state as light is the source of all life, whatever light shines on will have the characteristics of vitality. In this series of photos, we illuminated the sculpture with different kinds of light. The light we chose if a light with sunset colors, representing the cycle of night and day.

Digital Sculpture: Ladder of real estate (By Junha)

In the artwork, eyeballs are stuck on to the purple cube looking at the glowing cube. eyes on the cube represent the people craving for sunlight. Cubes are the limited space in the city. Glowing light is the right that everyone should have by having sunlight. The art work is an expression of feelings on how light in the city displays class struggles.
